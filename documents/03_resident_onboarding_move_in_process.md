# Resident Onboarding and Move-In Process

This document describes the required steps after an applicant is approved and before the resident receives keys. The leasing team should use this process to ensure every move-in is complete, documented, and consistent.

## Approval to Move-In Timeline

Once an application is approved, the leasing office sends a move-in checklist within 1 business day. The applicant must complete all move-in requirements no later than 2 business days before the scheduled move-in date.

If requirements are not completed on time, Northstar may delay the move-in appointment or release the apartment to another applicant depending on the application agreement.

## Required Move-In Items

Keys cannot be released until all of the following are complete:

1. Lease and addenda signed by all adult residents
2. Move-in funds paid in full
3. Renter’s insurance verified
4. Required utilities activated
5. Identity verification completed
6. Pet or assistance animal documentation completed, if applicable
7. Parking registration completed, if applicable
8. Move-in appointment scheduled
9. Welcome packet acknowledged in resident portal

## Move-In Funds

Move-in funds may include:

| Charge | Typical Amount |
|---|---:|
| First full month’s rent | Based on lease |
| Prorated rent | Based on move-in date |
| Security deposit | $500 to 1.5x monthly rent |
| Administrative fee | $175 |
| Amenity activation fee | $75 |
| Trash setup fee | $25 |
| Package locker setup fee | $10 |
| Parking permit | $25 per permit |
| Pet fee | $300 per pet |
| Pet deposit | $250 per pet |
| Monthly pet rent | $35 per pet |

Certified funds may be required if move-in occurs within 72 hours of approval.

## Prorated Rent Calculation

Prorated rent is calculated using the actual number of days in the move-in month.

Formula:

Monthly base rent divided by days in month, multiplied by number of lease days in that month.

Example:

- Monthly rent: $1,550
- Move-in date: June 20
- Days in June: 30
- Lease days in June: 11
- Prorated rent: $1,550 / 30 x 11 = $568.33

## Lease Signing

All adult residents must sign the lease and required addenda electronically unless management approves paper signing.

Common addenda include:

- Utility billing addendum
- Community rules
- Pest control addendum
- Mold prevention addendum
- Renters insurance addendum
- Package locker addendum
- Parking addendum
- Pet addendum
- Bed bug addendum
- Smoke-free addendum, if applicable

Unsigned addenda delay move-in.

## Renter’s Insurance Requirement

Residents must maintain renter’s insurance with at least $100,000 in personal liability coverage.

The policy must include:

- Resident name
- Apartment address
- Policy effective date on or before move-in date
- Minimum liability coverage
- Northstar Residential Management listed as interested party
- Ownership entity listed as interested party if required by the property

Residents who fail to provide proof may be enrolled in a landlord liability program at a monthly cost of $14.95. The landlord liability program does not protect the resident’s personal belongings.

## Utility Setup

Residents must activate required utilities before move-in. Required utilities vary by property but may include electricity, gas, internet, water, sewer, or trash.

The leasing office provides provider information in the move-in checklist.

Residents must upload confirmation numbers or screenshots showing active service. Utility confirmation must match the apartment address and resident name.

## Move-In Appointment

Move-in appointments are scheduled Monday through Saturday during office hours. Same-day move-ins are not guaranteed.

Appointments are usually scheduled in 30-minute windows. Residents should arrive on time with photo ID.

If the resident arrives more than 15 minutes late, the appointment may be rescheduled.

## Key Release

At move-in, staff may release:

- Apartment keys
- Mailbox key
- Amenity fob
- Gate remote
- Parking permit
- Package locker instructions
- Smart lock setup link

|---|---:|

| Mailbox key | $35 |
| Amenity fob | $75 |
| Gate remote | $85 |
| Parking permit | $50 |

## Move-In Condition Form

Residents must complete the move-in condition form within 48 hours after key release.

The form should document:

- Wall marks
- Flooring damage
- Appliance issues
- Cabinet damage
- Countertop damage
- Window or blind issues
- Bathroom condition
- Missing items
- Safety concerns

Photos should be uploaded through the resident portal.

The move-in condition form is not a maintenance request. If a repair is needed, the resident must submit a separate maintenance request.

## First Week Resident Check-In

The leasing team sends a first-week check-in message 5 to 7 days after move-in.

The check-in asks about:

- Portal access
- Maintenance needs
- Package locker setup
- Parking or access issues
- Utility billing questions
- Community rule questions

Any unresolved issue should be routed to the appropriate team within 1 business day.
