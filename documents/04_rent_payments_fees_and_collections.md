# Rent Payments, Fees, and Collections

This document explains Northstar Residential Management’s standard rent collection process, fee schedule, delinquency workflow, and payment plan rules.

## Rent Due Date and Grace Period

Rent is due on the 1st day of each month. A grace period applies through the 3rd day of the month unless local law or the lease says otherwise.

Rent is late beginning at 12:00 AM on the 4th day of the month.

Residents are responsible for paying on time even if they do not receive a reminder, invoice, email, text message, or portal notification.

## Monthly Billing Statement

Monthly charges are posted to the resident ledger by the 25th day of the prior month when possible.

Common monthly charges include:

- Base rent
- Pet rent
- Parking rent
- Storage rent
- Amenity fees
- Utility billing
- Pest control fee
- Valet trash fee
- Package locker fee
- Month-to-month premium
- Renters insurance non-compliance fee

Residents should review the ledger before the 1st and report billing questions immediately.

## Accepted Payment Methods

Preferred payment method is ACH through the resident portal.

Payment methods:

| Method | Notes |
|---|---|
| ACH | Preferred; lowest processing cost |
| Debit card | Processor convenience fee applies |
| Credit card | Processor convenience fee applies |
| Certified check | Accepted only when required or approved |
| Money order | Accepted only at properties that allow it |
| Cash | Not accepted |

Northstar employees may not accept cash payments.

## Late Fee Workflow

Standard delinquency workflow:

| Date | Action |
|---|---|
| 1st | Rent due |
| 2nd | Friendly reminder sent by email or SMS |
| 4th | Late fee posted and delinquency notice sent |
| 5th | Leasing team calls residents with unpaid balances |
| 7th | Notice to pay or vacate prepared where permitted |
| 10th | Manager review of delinquent accounts |
| 15th | Legal referral review if no payment plan or resolution |
| 20th | Final internal collection review |

The exact legal notice timeline depends on local law.

## Partial Payments

Partial payments may be accepted before legal referral unless restricted by property policy or legal strategy.

Acceptance of a partial payment does not waive the right to collect the remaining balance, charge late fees, issue notices, or enforce the lease unless a written agreement says otherwise.

After a file has been referred to legal counsel, partial payments may be rejected unless approved by the property manager and legal counsel.

## Payment Plans

Payment plans are discretionary and must be documented in writing.

Payment plan eligibility:

- Resident has made at least one on-time payment in the prior 3 months
- Resident is not already on a failed payment plan
- Resident communicates before legal referral
- Resident can pay at least 40% of the balance upfront
- Remaining balance can be paid within 30 days

Standard payment plan structure:

- 40% due immediately
- 30% due within 14 days
- Final 30% due within 30 days

Payment plans do not waive future rent. Next month’s rent must still be paid on time.

## Returned Payment Process

If a payment is returned:

1. The returned payment fee is posted.
2. Any late fees caused by the return are posted.
3. Staff sends a returned payment notice.
4. Resident must repay using certified funds if required.
5. Repeated returned payments trigger certified funds restriction.

After 3 returned payments in a 12-month period, the resident must pay by certified funds for 6 months.

## Ledger Disputes

Residents who dispute a charge must submit the dispute in writing through the resident portal or leasing office.

The dispute should include:

- Charge being disputed
- Reason for dispute
- Supporting documents
- Desired resolution

Management reviews ledger disputes within 5 business days.

A pending dispute does not pause rent obligations unless management confirms in writing.

## Collections After Move-Out

Unpaid balances after move-out may include:

- Rent
- Late fees
- Utilities
- Cleaning charges
- Damages
- Missing keys or fobs
- Lease break fees
- Concession repayment
- Legal fees where permitted
- Collection costs where permitted

Final account statements are prepared after move-out inspection and ledger reconciliation.

Balances unpaid after 30 days may be sent to a collection agency.
